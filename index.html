About

Hi! I'm Zayan Kamran, a dedicated web developer with a passion for crafting seamless and engaging web experiences. With a solid background in computer science and several years of hands-on experience, I specialize in building responsive and user-friendly websites. I have a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of the latest web technologies and trends. Over the years, I have developed a wide range of websites, from simple static pages to complex web applications. My expertise lies in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but I am also proficient in various frameworks and libraries such as React, Angular, and Vue.js. I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills to stay ahead in this ever-evolving field.
field In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong sense of design and user experience. I believe that a website should not only function well but also look great and be easy to use. I enjoy collaborating with designers and other developers to bring creative ideas to life.
When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge with the community through blog posts and workshops. I am also an avid gamer and love to unwind with a good book or a challenging puzzle.
